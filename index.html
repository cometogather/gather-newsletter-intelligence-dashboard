Strategic Insights

Key observations from Q1 sends. These patterns should inform Q2 planning conversations with clients.

Re-engagement sequences outperforming. Clients who ran a 3-part re-engagement flow in Jan–Feb saw 22% average list reactivation. Recommend expanding to 8 more clients in Q2.

Subject line personalization lift is plateauing. First-name tokens now show diminishing returns (<1% incremental open rate). Testing company-name and role-based tokens for enterprise segments.

Mobile open share hit 71%. Three client templates still optimized for desktop-first layouts. Flag for redesign in Q2 roadmap conversations.

Wednesday 9–10am sends dominate. 14 of top 20 performing campaigns sent in this window. Capacity constraints emerging; need send-time diversification strategy.

Plain-text variants driving B2B click rates. For 6 SaaS/fintech clients, plain-text or minimal-HTML variants outperformed designed templates by up to 38% on CTR.

List hygiene is the #1 deliverability lever. Clients with quarterly suppression hygiene averaged 97.8% inbox placement vs 93.1% for those on ad-hoc cleanup schedules.

Q2 Recommendations

Prioritized action items for Q2 based on Q1 data. Assign owners before next team sync.

01 — High Priority

Launch send-time diversification program

Wednesday 9am is over-indexed. Model optimal send windows per client segment using Q1 engagement data to reduce concentration risk and improve deliverability.

02 — High Priority

Expand re-engagement sequence offering

3-part reactivation flow showed 22% list recovery rate in Q1. Package as a standalone add-on SKU and pitch to 8 identified clients with aging lists.

03 — High Priority

Remediate 4 at-risk deliverability accounts

Orion, BlueStar, Vantage Point, and Kestrel all have actionable issues. Assign a deliverability lead per account; complete remediations before April sends.

04 — Medium Priority

Roll out plain-text variant testing

B2B clients are seeing CTR lifts up to 38% on plain-text sends. Expand structured A/B testing to all 12 SaaS and fintech clients in Q2.

05 — Medium Priority

Upgrade 3 mobile-unfriendly templates

71% mobile open share makes desktop-first templates a conversion liability. Prioritize Orion, BlueStar, and Kestrel for responsive redesign in Q2.

06 — Medium Priority

Pilot BIMI rollout for 6 more clients

BIMI adoption is at 11/47. Brand logo in inbox improves open rates 5–8% in controlled studies. Target 6 clients with existing DMARC enforcement for Q2 pilot.
