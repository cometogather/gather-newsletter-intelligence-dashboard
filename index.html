The core idea: give your BD team content that opens doors.

The right article sent to the right prospect at the right moment in their context isn't a newsletter forward. It's a conversation opener. A credibility signal. A reason to reply. Pipeline Meeting Agenda Modifier

Reframe Your Pipeline Review Around Content Signal

Instead of "what's the status on this deal" — ask "what can we write that gives you a reason to reach out?"

For each active pursuit

Look up the target's role in the resonance heat map. Send the highest-resonance issue for that role before your next touch — it primes the conversation without feeling like a pitch.

For stalled relationships

A well-timed article forward is a low-friction reason to re-engage. It's not asking for a meeting — it's offering something useful. The reply rate is measurably higher than cold re-outreach.

For net-new targets

Add them to LinkedIn boost targeting before you reach out. When your content appears in their feed organically, your subsequent email reads as a warm follow-up — not a cold open.

For future content planning

Ask your BD team: "What could we write that you'd send to your three hardest prospects right now?" The answer tells you exactly what to publish next — and who's going to share it the moment it lands.
